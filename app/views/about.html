About Us

Our Story

Zabatnee is a joint venture company established in 2014 in accordance to law no. 8 for 1997 in the Arab Republic of Egypt with an authorized capital of LE 2.5 MN EGP. Zabatnee specializes in creating a turnkey platform that seamlessly integrates customers with activity providers; one of its first products is called Zabatnee.

This is achieved by creating a holistic environment that facilitates creation of an efficient marketplace that addresses stakeholders’ needs in a convenient, easy to use, secure and affordable manner.

Zabatnee is reservation platform that manages and automates the reservation and back-office processes of activity providers allowing them the capacity to better engage their customers. It is a convenient tool for customers to access the various activities available in their communities.
